A little about what we're making

Let's be real there are few things that are better in life than a deep-fried chicken wing. It is no secret that dunking a battered chicken wing into a bubbling pot of hot oil yields a delightfully crispy result. That said, deep-frying is always a bit of a process.

Not only is it messy (an apron is definitely required), but it's also such a headache to dispose of the dirty oil. So, if you're not in the mood to go through the whole procedure of deep-frying chicken wings (I don't blame you) but still want a super crunchy final product, there's one key ingredient that you need to add to your wings in order to yield a seemingly deep-fried snack from your oven.

Ingridients you'll need

  • Oil
  • Seasonings
  • Wings

How to make it

  1. Combine the oil and seasonings in a bag and shake to blend.
  2. Add the chicken wings to the bag, seal and shake to coat.
  3. Arrange the wings on a baking sheet and bake until crisp and cooked through.
